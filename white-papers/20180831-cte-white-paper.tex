\section{Background}
During the 2012-2013 academic year, I worked closely with Technology / Engineering Teacher Rich Monagle and Interim Assistant Superintendent Dick Robbat to develop a comprehensive plan for education involving science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM) in the Winchester public schools, grades K-12. 

\subsection{Technology}
The word `technology' is fraught. It comes from the Greek word $\tau\acute{\epsilon}\chi\eta\nu$, meaning both {\em art} and {\em craft}, and the Greek suffix $\lambda o\gamma\acute{\iota}\alpha$, meaning {\em system of} --- hence the English definition: {\em the practical application of knowledge, or a capability so enabled, or a specialized aspect of it}.
